Software Developer & Researcher

Debmalya Sur

Senior Engineer at Samsung Research Institute Bangalore, focused on on-device AI, computer vision, applied machine learning, and production-grade software systems.

I owned the end-to-end on-device machine learning pipeline for Samsung Galaxy S24 Dress Room's commercially launched SuggestStyle feature, covering model development, optimization, deployment, and production integration.

My work includes lightweight NanoDet-based object detection, aesthetics assessment, personalized prompt generation from Samsung Account preference signals, and ONNX/model-compression optimization that reduced a production model from 8MB to 2MB while reaching 20ms end-to-end on-device inference.

I am also developing a CLIP-based vision-language classification system with Samsung Ads for SFW/NSFW ad categorization, targeting 95% accuracy, low false rates, and major reduction in manual review. My research includes federated learning for heart failure prediction, IoMT security in Elsevier's Blockchain: Research and Applications, and AIR 501 in GATE CS 2022.
